This document describes how to install J9 on a Sidearm.

After installing Visual Age (aka "Websphere") Device Developer
on your Windows machine, copy the following files to the Sidearm's
/j9 directory:

lib/jclMax/classes.zip from 

C:\Program Files\IBM\Device Developer\wsdd5.0\ive\runtimes\common\ive\lib\jclMax

lib/prnsnlmot.jar from

C:\Program Files\IBM\Device Developer\wsdd5.0\ive\runtimes\linux\common\ive\lib\jclMax

lib/rmip.zip from 

C:\Program Files\IBM\Device Developer\wsdd5.0\ive\runtimes\common\ive\lib

bin/*.so,j9 from

C:\Program Files\IBM\Device Developer\wsdd5.0\ive\runtimes\mvl\arm\ive\bin

lib/*.a from 

C:\Program Files\IBM\Device Developer\wsdd5.0\ive\runtimes\mvl\arm\ive\bin\lib
